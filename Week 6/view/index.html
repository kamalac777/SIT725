About Nepal

  • Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, sharing borders with India and China. The country is best known for its diverse culture, temples, monasteries, and natural landmarks like Mount Everest, the world’s highest peak.
  • With over 100 ethnic groups, Nepal is rich in customs, traditions, and languages. Visitors can witness a unique blend of Hindu and Buddhist influences, reflected in the country's architecture, festivals, and art.
  • Kathmandu, the capital city, is a vibrant hub for tourism, boasting UNESCO World Heritage Sites, ancient palaces, and bustling markets.
  • Nepal's diverse landscapes range from the majestic Himalayas to lush jungles. Whether it's adventure, culture, or spirituality, Nepal offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.
